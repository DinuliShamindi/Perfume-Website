Our Beginnings

Founded in the heart of Grasse, LORITO began as a small family apothecary dedicated to the healing power of botanicals. Over generations, our passion evolved from medicinal concoctions to the fine art of perfumery.

We believe that a fragrance is not merely an accessory but an invisible aura that defines one's presence. Every bottle of LORITO is a testament to patience, precision, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

The Process

From Earth to Bottle

Sourcing

We travel the globe to select only the rarest and most potent raw materials, ensuring ethical harvesting.

Distillation

Our master distillers use centuries-old techniques to extract the purest oils, preserving their soul.

Maturation

Each blend is aged in French oak barrels for months, allowing the notes to harmonize perfectly.
